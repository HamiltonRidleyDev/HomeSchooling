Part 3: What Would YOU Do?

Read each story (or have someone read it to you). What should happen?

Story 1: You walk into the kitchen and see a big mess on the counter, but you didn't make it. What should you do?

Story 2: Your brother or sister is trying to build something and it keeps falling down. They look frustrated. What should you do?

Story 3: You're playing a game and you really want to win, but your sibling is about to cry because they keep losing. What should you do?

Story 4: You accidentally broke something. Nobody saw it happen. What should you do?

Part 4: My Family Rules

Now think about everything above. What rules or sayings should our family live by? Try to come up with at least 3! You can write them, draw them, or tell a grown-up and they'll write them for you.
