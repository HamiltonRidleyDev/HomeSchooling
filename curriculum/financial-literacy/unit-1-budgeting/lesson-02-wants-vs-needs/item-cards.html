Item Cards

Cut these out. Sort each card into NEEDS, GRAY AREA, or WANTS.

How to use: Print all pages. Cut along card borders. Shuffle the deck. Sort each card onto the sorting mat. There are no perfectly right answers for many of these — that's the point!
Hint: Green-bordered cards are usually needs. Orange are usually wants. Yellow are gray area. But don't tell the kids that — let them figure it out!
