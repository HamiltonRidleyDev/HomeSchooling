Situation Cards

These change the context. Read one aloud, then ask: does anything move?

How to use: After the group has sorted the item cards into NEEDS, GRAY AREA, and WANTS, read 3–4 of these situation cards aloud. After each one, the group looks at all three piles and decides if any cards should move to a different pile. Physically pick up cards and move them!
🌾
Country Living
You live on a farm 20 miles from the nearest town. There's no bus, no Uber, no subway. The closest grocery store is a 30-minute drive.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: car, internet, bicycle, public transit...
🌇
Big City Life
You live in New York City with great public transit. A subway pass gets you everywhere. Parking costs $400/month and traffic is terrible.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: car, public transit pass, car insurance...
👶
New Parent
You have a 2-year-old child who depends on you for everything. You need to work to pay bills, but someone has to watch the baby.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: childcare, life insurance, bigger home, car...
💻
Remote Worker
You work from home full-time. Your entire job depends on being online. No internet = no paycheck. But you never commute.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: internet, laptop, work clothes, car, gym...
🩹
Medical Condition
You have a medical condition that requires daily medication, monthly doctor visits, and a special diet. Without treatment, you can't function.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: health insurance, vitamins, prescription medicine, healthy groceries...
💵
Job Loss
You just lost your job. You have some savings but need to make them last. You're applying for new jobs every day but it could take months.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: streaming, eating out, gym, internet (job searching), emergency savings...
🏅
Olympic Athlete
You're training for the Olympics. You practice 6 hours a day. Your body is your career. You need specific food, equipment, and recovery.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: gym membership, vitamins, healthy food, sports fees, spa/massage...
☀️
Desert Heat
You live somewhere it's 100°F+ in the summer for months. Last year, dozens of people were hospitalized from heat. There's no shade and no breeze.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: air conditioning, water, electricity bill...
🎓
College Student
You're in college full-time. Every class requires online assignments, research papers, and video lectures. The library closes at 9 PM.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: laptop, internet, books, college education...
🎭
Professional Gamer
You earn your living as a professional video game streamer. Your gaming setup IS your office. Your fans pay to watch you play.
Look at your piles. Does anything need to move?
Think about: gaming console, internet, streaming service, computer...
