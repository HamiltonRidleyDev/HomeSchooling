Budget Bucks! Print on cardstock if possible. Cut along the edges. Give each kid their "paycheck" at the start of the exercise. As they make budget choices, they hand money back. Makes budgeting physical and real!
Tip: Print 2-3 pages depending on how much money you need. The 15-year-old with $4,000 needs more bills than the 6-year-old with $500.
