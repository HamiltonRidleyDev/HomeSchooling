🛡 Insurance — Your Financial Shield

What is insurance? It's a deal: you pay a small amount every month, and if something terrible happens (fire, car crash, medical emergency), the insurance company pays most of the cost. Without it, one bad event can completely wreck your finances. A "deductible" is the amount YOU pay first before insurance kicks in.

Renter's Insurance

Skip It
$0/mo — but if there's a fire or theft, YOU pay thousands out of pocket.
