Surprise Cards

Life throws curveballs. Your past choices determine how hard they hit.

How these work: Draw a card mid-exercise. Your cost depends on what you chose earlier — insurance, savings, car type, etc. The kid who paid for better insurance or saved an emergency fund comes out better. That's the whole lesson.
Ages K-4th: Parent reads the card and picks the simple outcome.
Ages 5th+: Kid finds their own outcome based on their budget choices.
Expense Cards — Your choices determine the damage
💥Expense — Car
Car Accident!
Someone rear-ended you at a stoplight. Your car needs repairs and you need to get to work.
What do you owe?
No car (walk/bike/bus)$0 — doesn't affect you!
Car + Premium insurance (low deductible)-$250
Car + Standard insurance-$1,000
Car + Basic/liability-only insurance-$2,500 (you pay for your own repairs)
Lesson: Better insurance costs more per month but protects you when it matters. Liability-only means they don't fix YOUR car.
🔥Expense — Home
Kitchen Fire!
A grease fire damaged your kitchen and destroyed some of your belongings — electronics, clothes, furniture.
What do you owe?
Have renter's/home insurance-$500 (deductible only — insurance covers the rest)
NO renter's insurance-$4,500 (replace everything yourself)
Lesson: Renter's insurance costs $15-25/month. Without it, one bad event can wipe out months of savings. That $15 just saved you $4,000.
🏥Expense — Health
Broken Arm!
You slipped and broke your arm. You need an ER visit, X-rays, a cast, and follow-up appointments.
What do you owe?
Premium health insurance (low deductible)-$150 (copays only)
Standard health insurance-$800
Basic / high deductible health insurance-$2,500
No health insurance-$7,500 (full ER bill)
Lesson: An ER visit without insurance can cost $5,000-$10,000+. Health insurance is expensive but medical debt is the #1 cause of bankruptcy in America.
📱Expense — Tech
Phone Screen Shattered!
Your phone fell out of your pocket and the screen cracked. You need it for work and daily life.
What do you owe?
Budget phone plan (cheaper phone)-$80 (screen repair)
Flagship phone, no protection plan-$250 (screen repair)
Have emergency fund with $500+Pay from emergency fund — annoying but manageable
No emergency fundPut it on credit card — now you're paying interest
Lesson: Emergency funds exist for exactly this. Without one, small emergencies become debt spirals.
🚗Expense — Car
Car Won't Start!
Your car's transmission is failing. You can't drive it. You need it for work.
What do you owe?
New car (under warranty)$0 — warranty covers it!
Reliable used car + premium insurance with roadside-$400 (some repair cost)
Older used car, basic insurance-$1,800 (transmission repair)
No car$0 — doesn't affect you
Lesson: Cheap cars save on payments but cost more in repairs. New cars cost more monthly but come with warranties. There's a trade-off either way.
🕶Expense — Home
Apartment Break-In!
Someone broke into your apartment and stole your laptop, TV, and some jewelry. You feel violated and need to replace essentials.
What do you owe?
Have renter's insurance-$500 deductible (insurance replaces items)
No renter's insurance-$2,800 (replace laptop + essentials yourself)
Lesson: Another reason renter's insurance is worth every penny. It covers theft, fire, water damage, and more.
💼Expense — Income
Hours Cut at Work!
Business is slow and your boss cut your hours. Your income drops for 2 months.
Impact:
Your income this month-25% of your monthly take-home
Have emergency fund with 1+ month of expensesTight but you survive — this is what it's for
No emergency fundYou can't pay all your bills. Which ones do you skip?
Have a side hustleYour extra income covers the gap!
Lesson: This is why emergency funds AND multiple income streams matter. A side hustle isn't just extra cash — it's a safety net.
💍Expense — Social
Best Friend's Wedding!
Your best friend is getting married in another city. You need travel, hotel, outfit, and a gift. You don't want to miss it.
What do you owe?
Full cost (flights + hotel + outfit + gift)-$600
Budget version (drive, share room, modest gift)-$250
Can your savings/emergency fund handle it?This is a WANT, not a NEED. Savings or skip?
Lesson: Not all expenses are emergencies. Some are choices. Budgeting means you can say YES to things that matter without going into debt.
Windfall Cards — Good things happen too
💼Windfall
Work Bonus!
Your boss noticed your hard work and gave you a $500 bonus this month.
What will you do with it?
Put it all in savings/investments+$500 to savings
Split it — half save, half spend+$250 savings + something fun
Spend it allFun now, gone tomorrow
Pay off debt with itReduces interest you'll pay!
Lesson: What you do with EXTRA money reveals your financial habits. The 50/50 split is a great balanced approach.
💰
Tax Refund!
You filed your taxes and got $800 back from the government.
What will you do with it?
Build emergency fund+$800 — now you have a cushion
Invest itAt 10%/yr, this becomes ~$2,000 in 10 years
Pay off credit card debtSaves you hundreds in interest
Treat yourselfFeels great today. Write down what you'd buy.
Lesson: A tax refund means you overpaid taxes all year — it's YOUR money coming back. Using it strategically compounds over time.
🚀Windfall
Side Hustle Blew Up!
Your side hustle had a great month — a big order, a viral post, or a lucky break.
One-time boost:
Extra income this month+$750
But you could reinvest $300 into the business to grow it...Spend $300 now → potentially earn more every month
Lesson: Entrepreneurs face this choice constantly — take profit now or reinvest to grow? Both are valid. What's your strategy?
🎁Windfall
Birthday Money!
Family and friends gave you $300 for your birthday. It's a gift — no strings attached.
Your choice:
You decide!+$300
Write down exactly what you'd do with it:_____________
Lesson: Gifts are a great test of your financial priorities. What you do with free money shows what you really value.
📈Windfall
Investment Growth!
If you chose to invest any money, your investments had a great month.
Result:
You invested money monthlyYour portfolio grew 8% this quarter — it's working!
You didn't invest anythingYou missed out — your money in a checking account earned $0.02
Lesson: Money in a checking account loses value to inflation. Invested money grows. Time in the market beats timing the market.
🤝Windfall
Community Payback!
If you donated to charity or volunteered, someone you helped connected you with a new opportunity.
Result:
You gave to charity / helped your communityYou get a referral that earns +$400 this month
You didn't give or volunteerNo effect — but think about what giving could open up
Lesson: Generosity isn't just about the money — it builds relationships and reputation. What goes around often comes around.
Temptation & Modern Life Cards — Real-world traps and opportunities
👟Temptation
Limited-Edition Sneaker Drop!
The shoes everyone wants just dropped. $280. Your friends are all buying them. They'll be worth $400 on resale... maybe.
What happens?
You buy them (no emergency fund)-$280 on credit card (+ interest next month)
You buy them (have emergency fund)-$280 from savings. Worth it?
You resist$0. Your savings stay intact. The hype dies in 2 weeks.
Lesson: Impulse purchases feel urgent but rarely are. The "limited edition" pressure is a marketing trick.
📈Modern Risk
Crypto / Meme Stock Hype!
Everyone online says this crypto coin is "going to the moon." It's up 300% this month.
What happens?
You invested $200 last month — it's now $800! Do you sell?If you sell: +$600 profit. Smart.
You hold instead of sellingFlip a coin. Heads = +$1,200. Tails = -$150 (crash to almost nothing).
You didn't invest$0 effect. FOMO hurts but your money is safe.
Lesson: If it sounds too good to be true, it usually is. Taking profits is never wrong. Gambling isn't investing.
📱Temptation
Phone Upgrade Pressure!
Your phone works fine but the new model just came out. All your friends have it. The carrier offers $0 down — just $45/month for 24 months.
What happens?
You upgrade-$45/month for 2 years (total: $1,080)
You keep your current phone$0. It still works. You saved $1,080.
You buy a refurbished model-$300 one-time. Saved $780 vs new.
Lesson: "$0 down" isn't free — it's a 2-year commitment. Calculate the TOTAL cost, not the monthly cost.
⚠️Modern Trap
Scam Text / Phishing Attack!
You get a text: "Your bank account has been compromised. Click here to verify your identity immediately."
What happens?
You learned about scams (Digital Safety lesson)$0 — you recognized it and deleted it.
You clicked the link and entered your info-$500 stolen from your account. Takes 2-3 weeks to recover (if you're lucky).
Lesson: Urgency + fear = scam. Your bank will NEVER text you a link. When in doubt, call the number on your actual bank card.
🔁Temptation
Subscription Creep!
You signed up for a few free trials. You forgot to cancel. You're now paying for 4 services you barely use.
What happens?
You track your subscriptions monthly$0 — you caught it.
You don't track-$65/month in forgotten subscriptions. That's $780/year!
Lesson: Set a calendar reminder when ANY free trial starts. Audit your subscriptions every month. The companies are counting on you forgetting.
🤝Social Pressure
Friend Asks for Money!
Your close friend is in a tough spot and asks to borrow $300. They promise to pay you back next month.
What happens?
You lend it AND they pay back$0 net, friendship intact.
You lend it and they DON'T pay back-$300 and an awkward friendship.
You say no (or offer $50 you can afford to lose)$0 to -$50. Friendship tested but finances safe.
Lesson: Never lend money you can't afford to lose. If you lend to a friend, consider it a gift in your mind — if they pay it back, great. If not, you're not surprised or resentful.
🌐Positive Opportunity
Referral / Networking Bonus!
Someone you helped in your community recommends you for a freelance gig / job opportunity.
What happens?
You gave to charity or volunteered+$500 one-time opportunity. Generosity opened a door.
You didn't give or connect with communityNo effect. You never met this person.
Lesson: Your network is your net worth. Giving — time, money, or help — builds relationships that come back in unexpected ways.
🎥Modern Trap
Viral Side Hustle "Course"!
An influencer promises: "Buy my $197 course and learn to make $10K/month from home!" Testimonials look amazing.
What happens?
You buy it-$197. The "course" is 3 YouTube videos you could've found for free.
You research first$0. A quick Google search reveals it's mostly hype.
Lesson: If someone's making money selling a course about making money... they're making money from YOU, not from the method they're teaching. Be skeptical of "easy money" promises.
