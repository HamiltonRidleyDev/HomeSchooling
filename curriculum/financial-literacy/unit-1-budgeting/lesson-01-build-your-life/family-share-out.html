🏠 Family Share-Out

The Most Important 10 Minutes

After everyone has completed their Financial Autobiography, gather together. This is not a test or a presentation — it’s a conversation. Each person shares one answer they’re proud of or that surprised them. Listen without judging. This is the moment where the numbers become personal.

💡 How It Works

1
Sit together — living room, dinner table, wherever your family talks.
2
Each person picks ONE answer from their autobiography to read aloud. (2 minutes per person)
3
After each person shares, family can ask ONE follow-up question or say what they appreciated.
4
After everyone has shared, ask the whole family: “What’s one money rule we could adopt as a FAMILY?”
