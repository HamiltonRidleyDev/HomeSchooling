Career Cards

Pick your job. Your job determines your income. Your income shapes your life.

How to use: Pick a career that interests you — OR draw one randomly (cut cards out, flip face-down, draw). Your career determines your monthly take-home pay. This is what you'll use for your budget.
Younger kids (K-4th): Just look at the picture and the green number — that's your monthly money.
Older kids (5th+): Notice the difference between what you earn (gross) and what you take home (net) after taxes.
