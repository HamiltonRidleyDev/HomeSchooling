The 50 / 30 / 20 Rule

Financial experts suggest splitting your income roughly like this:

50% — Needs Housing, utilities, food, transportation, insurance
30% — Wants Entertainment, dining out, clothing, phone upgrades, hobbies
20% — Savings & Future Savings, emergency fund, investing, debt payoff
