Notes & Teachable Moments

Write down anything that came up — a money question, a real-world situation, something from the news.

Keep It Positive

Keep it positive and SHORT. This isn't a lecture — it's a celebration of progress. If a kid overspent, ask "what would you do differently?" not "why did you spend that?" The goal is self-awareness, not guilt.

Consistency matters more than perfection. Even a 5-minute check-in is better than skipping the month. Put it on the calendar — same day each month — and protect the time.
