💡 Did You Know?

📅

Tax Freedom Day: The average American works from January 1 to about April 16 just to earn enough to pay all their taxes for the year. Everything you earn after that is yours to keep. That date is called "Tax Freedom Day."

👴

Why Social Security Exists: Social Security was created in 1935 during the Great Depression so elderly people wouldn't have to work until the day they died. Before it existed, about half of all seniors lived in poverty.

📨

150 Million Returns: The IRS processes about 150 million individual tax returns every single year. That's almost one for every two people in the country.
