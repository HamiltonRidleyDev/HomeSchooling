The "Hockey Stick" — How Compound Growth Actually Looks

This Is EXACTLY What Happens With Investing

For years it feels like nothing is happening. Your account barely grows. It's tempting to quit, spend the money, do something else.

Then suddenly, it takes off. The growth in the last 10-15 years of investing dwarfs everything that came before. The people who gave up during the flat part missed the explosion.

The secret? Patience. Just keep going. The penny didn't look like much on Day 15. But everything it did in those boring early days made the explosion possible.
