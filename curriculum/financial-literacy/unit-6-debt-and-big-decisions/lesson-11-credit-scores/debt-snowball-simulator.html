Debt Snowball Simulator

Escape the Debt Trap
You have $12,000 in debt across 3 sources. How you pay it off changes EVERYTHING.
Name
Date

Most adults have multiple debts at the same time. The way you attack them makes a huge difference in how fast you're free and how much you pay in interest. Two popular strategies:

Strategy 1: The Snowball Method

Pay minimums on everything, then throw ALL extra money at the smallest debt first. When it's gone, roll that payment into the next smallest. Like a snowball getting bigger as it rolls.

  • Advantage: Quick wins keep you motivated
  • Best for: People who need momentum
Strategy 2: The Avalanche Method

Pay minimums on everything, then throw ALL extra money at the highest interest debt first. Mathematically saves the most money.

  • Advantage: Saves the most in interest
  • Best for: People who are disciplined and patient
