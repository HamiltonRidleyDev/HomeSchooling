Power Sentence Techniques
  1. Replace vague words with specific ones — big → towering, massive, enormous
  2. Use strong verbs — walked → trudged, sprinted, shuffled
  3. Add sensory details — sight, sound, smell, touch, taste
  4. Show emotions through actions, not labels — don't say "sad," show what sadness looks like
