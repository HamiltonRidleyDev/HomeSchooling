My Opening Statement

30 seconds. Memorize it. This is your first impression — make it count. State your position clearly and give your strongest reason right away.

Debate Tips:
  • Look at your audience, not the floor.
  • Speak clearly and at a steady pace — rushing sounds nervous.
  • Say “You said X, but actually…” to show you’re listening to the other side.
  • End with your strongest point, not your weakest.
