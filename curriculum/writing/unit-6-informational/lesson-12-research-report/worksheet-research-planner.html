Section 3: My Notes

Organize your notes by question. Write everything IN YOUR OWN WORDS. If you use an exact quote, put it in quotation marks.

The Paraphrasing Test: Read the source. Close the book (or look away from the screen). Now write what you learned in your own words. If you can’t do it without looking, read it again until you understand it — then try again.
