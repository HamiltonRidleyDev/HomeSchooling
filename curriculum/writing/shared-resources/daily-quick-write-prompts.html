Daily Quick Write Prompts

10 Minutes. No Stopping. Just Write.

Set a timer for 10 minutes. Pick a prompt. Write without stopping. Don't erase, don't edit, don't worry about spelling. The goal is FLOW, not perfection. These are never graded.

Note for Parents:

Let kids pick their own prompt. If they hate all the options, they can write about whatever they want. The only rule: 10 minutes, no stopping.
