Section 1: Study the Funnel

Read this annotated example. Notice how each sentence zooms in closer.

Example: Financial Literacy in Schools

BROAD "Money touches every part of adult life — where you live, what you eat, how you get around."

NARROW "Yet most teenagers graduate without ever learning to manage a budget."

THESIS "Teaching financial literacy before age 18 is the most impactful thing schools could do."

Primary (K–1): Do topics 1 and 2 only. Write just 2 sentences: a broad sentence and your main point. Skip the narrow sentence.
Elementary (3rd–4th): Do topics 1, 2, and 3. Write all 3 sentences for each.
Middle & High School: Do all 5 topics.
