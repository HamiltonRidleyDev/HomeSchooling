The 8 Hook Techniques
1. Startling Fact — a surprising number or statistic
2. Question — a question the reader wants to answer
3. Bold Claim — a strong, attention-grabbing statement
4. Anecdote — a short, real story
5. Dialogue — start with someone talking
6. Action — drop into the middle of something happening
7. Sensory Image — paint a vivid picture with the senses
8. Mystery / Surprise — create a gap the reader needs to fill
